Pancake In a Mug

Pancake In a Mug

Ingrediants

  • Microwave safe mug
  • 1 Tbs. Butter
  • 3/4 Tbs. Sugar
  • 2 Tbs. Milk
  • 3 Tbs. Flour
  • 1/4 Tsp. Baking Powder
  • 1/8 Tsp. Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Put butter in a microwave safe bowl.
  2. Melt in microwave for 25 seonds.
  3. Then add sugar, milk, flour, baking powder and vanilla extract.
  4. Mix together just till combined. Do not over mix.
  5. Then bake in the microwave for 1 minute. Do not do it longer than this or it will damage your food.
  6. Then put some butter and syrup on top and eat up!
